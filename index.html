About

I was born at Agoo, La Union but grew up at Al-alinao Norte, Naguilian, La Union. I studied at Al-alinao Elamentary School. I was First in Honor when I was in Kindergarten I to Grade II, Second in Honor starting Grade II to Grade V, and Salutatorian in Grade VI. I joined Girls Scout of the Philippines starting Grade I till Grade VI.

I went to Baguio City after graduating in Elementary. I studied at Baguio City National High School - Main. I was in Star Sections. Star Sections are sections for regular students who have high average. I still joined the Girls Scout of the Philippines, although only on my first year.

Now, I'm studying at the University of the Cordilleras. I am taking the course of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Network and Security Track. I am currently a 2nd Year Student. I was one of the Dean's Lister when I was on my First year, First Term and Third Term. I am one of the College Scholar on my Second year First Term

I like Anime. My favorite anime is Sword Art Online, Second is Guilty Crown. I like color Blue, Black, White and sometimes Red. I like sweets and chocolates. I also like anything cute like Chibis, Pandas, and Puppies. I like romance, comedy, mysteries, music and art, too.

I only have few dislikes. I dislike wearing skirts. I also dislike anything that is dirty. I dislike peolpe who are arrogant, have no respect, have no manners and of course liars.

My hobbbies are watching anime or movies, reading manga or books, drawing or sketching, and sing. I also do cooking, cleaning, collecting stuff, taking pictures and playing piano.
